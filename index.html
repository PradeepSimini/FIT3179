Global Carbon Emissions

A visualisation by Pradeepsundar Premkumar Simini

Europe Carbon Emissions

The cartographic visualisation to the left shows carbon-dioxide emissions in Europe during 1990 normalized by country population. The country with the highest emissions per capita is Estonia while the least is Portugal. The highest total emissions in Europe was Germany.

Energy Consumption Trends

The Bubble plot to the right shows the relationship between the amount of total electric power consumed compared to the percentage of energy from fossil fuels. The visualisation shows a general trend indicating that countries with high energy consumption also have a high percentage of the energy generated from fossil fuels.
